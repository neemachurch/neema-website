About Neema Gospel Church

Neema Gospel Church is a Christ-centered ministry committed to discipleship, worship, and transforming lives.

Our Mission

We exist to help people experience the life-giving flow of God’s love and grace, and to equip them to live out their God-given purpose.

Our Vision

To love God, disciple, be equipped and empowered for service.

Our Values

God First, Reaching Out, Accepting our Assignment, Creating Community, Empowering Others.
