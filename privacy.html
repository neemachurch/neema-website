Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Neema Gospel Church (“NGC”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to honoring your privacy and protecting the personal information entrusted to us. As a faith-based ministry, we value integrity, transparency, and respect in all our interactions — both online and offline.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website or church services:

  • Personal details voluntarily submitted through forms (such as name, email address, and phone number).
  • Giving and donation-related information submitted through secure platforms (e.g., M-PESA).
  • Basic, non-identifiable analytics data used to understand website traffic and engagement.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for ministry and communication purposes, including:

  • Processing offerings, tithes, and donations securely.
  • Communicating church updates, events, services, and ministry opportunities.
  • Responding to inquiries, prayer requests, or support messages.
  • Improving our digital platforms and online church experience.

4. Media, Photos & Videos

During church services and events, photographs and videos may be taken for ministry, communication, and outreach purposes. These materials may be published on our website, social media platforms, or other church communication channels.

We strive to do this respectfully and in a manner consistent with our Christian values. If you wish for an image featuring you or your child to be removed, please contact us and we will respond promptly.

5. Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, remember preferences, and analyze site usage. These tools do not collect sensitive personal information and are used only to improve functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as YouTube (for sermon videos), Google services (for analytics or maps), and mobile payment providers. These services may collect limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Your data is never sold, traded, or shared for commercial purposes.

8. Children’s Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. Any information related to minors is handled with special care and only collected with parental or guardian consent where applicable.

9. Your Rights & Choices

You may request to access, update, or remove your personal information at any time by contacting us. We respect your choices and will honor your request where reasonably possible.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
