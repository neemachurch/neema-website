Find Your Place at Neema Gospel Church

Neema Gospel Church–Ruiru has a place for everyone. Whether you feel called to serve, lead, teach, or support, we have ministries where your gifts can manifest, thrive, and be a blessing to others.

Join us as we grow together in faith, fellowship, and purpose.

Explore our ministries and find where you belong.

Couple’s Ministry

At Neema Gospel Church–Ruiru, marriages are highly cherished and valued. Through regular couple’s meetings, we help married couples connect, grow in God’s Word, and build lasting relationships.

If you’re married, join this great couples fellowship today.

Generation Joshua

Generation Joshua is a movement of young people passionate about Jesus, purpose, and impact. Through vibrant worship, real conversations, and life-changing connections, youth are empowered to stand strong in faith and lead with courage.

Be part of a generation rising for God.

Destiny Generation

Destiny Generation is where children discover God’s love and purpose in a fun, safe, and faith-filled environment through engaging lessons, worship, and activities.

Join us in shaping young hearts for God’s Kingdom.

Ladies Ministry

Ladies Ministry is where women find strength, encouragement, and purpose. Through intimate gatherings, women grow together in God’s Word, support one another, and build meaningful relationships.

Step into a community of faith, growth, and sisterhood.

Men’s Ministry

Men’s Ministry is where men discover strength, purpose, and brotherhood through authentic conversations, shared experiences, and biblical teaching.

Join a community of courage, growth, and godly leadership.

Worship Ministry

The Worship Ministry leads the church into God’s presence through authentic, Spirit-led worship. Whether you sing, play an instrument, or serve behind the scenes, there’s a place for you.

Worship with excellence, unity, and purpose.

Widows Ministry

Widows Ministry is a sanctuary of compassion, strength, and renewed hope for women who have experienced the loss of a spouse. Through fellowship, prayer, and encouragement, widows find healing and community.

You are embraced, empowered, and never alone.
